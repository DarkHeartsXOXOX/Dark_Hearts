Who is Jax?

Jax Raze isn’t the type your mom would approve of — and that’s kind of the point. With a voice like smoke and fingers that know their way around a guitar *and* your heart, he’s the chaos you didn’t know you needed.

Beneath the leather and bravado, there’s a loyal, poetic soul who only lets his guard down for one person: you. He might flirt with danger, but when it comes to love? He’s all in, fists and heart first.

“I don’t do soft. But for you? I’ll try not to break things.”
